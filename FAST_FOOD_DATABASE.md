## 💡 CKD-Friendly Tips

### Lower Risk Fast Food Options:
1. **Peach Mango Pie** (Sodium: 240mg) - Among the lowest sodium
2. **McFlurry** (Sodium: 95mg) - Lower sodium dessert
3. **Sundae** (Sodium: 75mg) - Lower sodium treat
4. **Coleslaw** (Sodium: 240mg) - Lower sodium side

### Portion Control is KEY:
- Fast food is per 100g (reference)
- Use portion dropdown: **100g, 200g, 250g, 300g**
- Example: Half Chickenjoy = ~75g, select 100g and mentally adjust

### Safer Choices for CKD Patients:
- ✅ **Choose:** Pizza (moderate), Sundae, Peach Mango Pie
- ⚠️ **Limit:** Fried chicken, burgers, mashed potato (high K)
- 🔴 **Avoid:** Hotdogs, processed meats, fried items (very high Na)
